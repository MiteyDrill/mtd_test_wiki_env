It is fairly well known that Alice is a up and coming student in high school. She, like many other students, only think about surviving till the end of the school-year. Students require a balanced amount of skill, strength, and cunning, to maintain the perfect performance- up until the end of the school-year, when students are pitted against each other during the end of the year battle royale.

For Alice's final year, she needs more than strength to overcome the soon-to-be increasing amount of obstacles in her way.
